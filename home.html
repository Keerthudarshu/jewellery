Diamond Quality
Guaranteed

The 4Cs of diamond quality—cut, colour, clarity, and carat weight—are the essential factors to consider when choosing a diamond. They all contribute to its exceptional beauty and value.

01.

CUT QUALITY

×

The cut quality of a diamond is the most important factor in its brilliance and sparkle. A well-cut diamond reflects light more efficiently, making it brighter and more dazzling.

02.

COLOUR

+

Diamond colour refers to the presence or absence of colour in white diamonds. The less colour, the higher the grade and value of the diamond.

03.

CLARITY

+

Clarity measures the absence of inclusions and blemishes. Fewer, smaller inclusions mean higher clarity and greater value.

04.

CARAT WEIGHT

+

Carat weight measures a diamond's apparent size. One carat equals 200 milligrams. Larger diamonds are more rare and valuable.
